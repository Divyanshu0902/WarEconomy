Methodology and Interpretation

This analysis is correlation-focused and tracks stock movement around conflict lifecycle milestones.

  • Lifecycle stages: start, progression, end
  • Modes: absolute price or indexed baseline
  • Filters: US involvement and region

Correlation is not causation. Market-wide shocks, source timing differences, and survivorship bias may influence observed patterns.
